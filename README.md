## 📌 Problem

Many students face confusion after completing Class 10 or 12 due to:

* Lack of awareness about streams (Arts, Science, Commerce)
* Limited knowledge of available degree programs
* Unclear understanding of career opportunities
* Uncertainty about the value of higher education

This often leads to poor decisions, dropouts, or reliance on private institutions.

---

## 💡 Solution

**Career Buddy** is a digital platform that helps students make informed academic and career decisions by providing structured guidance, career insights, and accessible information.

---

## 🌟 Features

* 🎯 Career and stream guidance
* 🧭 Course-to-career mapping
* 🏫 Government college exploration
* 📊 Structured career insights
* 💬 User-friendly interface
